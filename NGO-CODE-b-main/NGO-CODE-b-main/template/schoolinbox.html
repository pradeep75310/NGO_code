📦 What is School-in-a-Box?

Our School-in-a-Box initiative is designed to bridge the educational divide by delivering comprehensive learning kits to children who lack access to formal schooling. From books and stationery to solar lamps and digital content, each box empowers a child to learn, grow, and dream.

📘 Learning Essentials

Every box contains textbooks, notebooks, pens, and pencils tailored to the child’s grade level.

🔦 Study Support

Solar-powered lamps help children study even in homes without electricity.

🎧 Audio Learning

Pre-loaded audio lessons bring classroom content to life in areas with no teachers.
