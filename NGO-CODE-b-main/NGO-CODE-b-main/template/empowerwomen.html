Empowering Women through Skill Development

Our new initiative in Rajasthan and Madhya Pradesh is empowering hundreds of women by providing them with vocational training in tailoring, crafts, and digital literacy.

Our Skill Development Initiative

In our mission to empower women in underserved communities, we have established skill development centers in Rajasthan and Madhya Pradesh. This program offers vocational training in tailoring, handicrafts, and digital literacy to help women become self-reliant and build sustainable livelihoods.

Tailoring

Women learn sewing, stitching, and garment making, opening doors for self-employment and income generation.

Crafts

Our programs teach the art of creating handmade crafts, such as pottery and textiles, enabling women to sell their products in local markets.

Digital Literacy

We provide training in basic computer skills, helping women access the digital world and pursue online work opportunities.

Program Impact

The impact of our skill development program is far-reaching. With over 1,200 women empowered, we are already seeing a change in the communities of Rajasthan and Madhya Pradesh.
