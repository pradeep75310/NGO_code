Mobile Health Vans: Bringing Healthcare to Remote Villages

Serving 10,000+ individuals across rural India with free checkups, essential vaccinations, and life-saving health education.

🚐 Healthcare That Travels

In areas where hospitals are miles away, our mobile health vans bring care directly to people’s doorsteps. With medical professionals on board, each visit delivers treatment, trust, and transformation across Uttar Pradesh, Odisha, and Assam.

🩺 Primary Services

Basic diagnostics, blood pressure & diabetes checks, maternal care, and more — all free of charge.

💉 Immunization Access

We ensure that no child or elder misses out on vaccines, no matter how remote their home is.

📚 Health Literacy

Awareness drives about nutrition, sanitation, and women’s health that empower entire communities.

🌟 Impact That Matters

This isn't just a service — it's a movement to ensure health equity in India's rural belts.
